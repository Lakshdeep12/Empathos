Mental Health: It's Okay to Not Be Okay

Mental health is just as important as physical health. It affects how we think, feel, and act. Understanding mental health is the first step toward better well-being.

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we handle stress, relate to others, and make choices. Mental health is important at every stage of life, from childhood and adolescence through adulthood.
